Percona is at the Kansas City Database Conference (September 9-11), connecting with developers, DBAs, and data professionals around open source databases and the technical problems you are working through in production.

- 📅 **September 9-11, 2026**
- 📍 **Kansas City, Missouri**
- 🏛 **Venue:** Kansas City Convention Center
- 🔗 **Official website:** https://www.kcdc.info/about

Percona is a **Gold sponsor** at KCDC. We'll be on the exhibit floor September 9-11 at the **Kansas City Convention Center** — a community-run database conference that draws developers, data professionals, and technology leaders from across the region. The program includes hands-on workshops, technical sessions, and time to talk through real software and data problems with people who run this stuff in production.

Stop by the **Percona booth** if you are attending. Talk databases, open source tooling, and practical approaches to performance, scalability, and reliability. Whether you are chasing a slow query, planning a migration, or comparing architectures, our team is there to answer questions and swap notes. Bring a specific problem if you have one — or just say hello.
