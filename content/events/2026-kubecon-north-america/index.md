Percona is a **Silver sponsor** at **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America**, November 9-12 in **Salt Lake City, Utah**. Find us in the **Solutions Showcase** to talk databases on Kubernetes — performance at scale, high availability, and what actually breaks when you run **MySQL**, **PostgreSQL**, or **MongoDB** in production clusters.

- 📅 **November 9-12, 2026**
- 📍 **Salt Lake City, Utah**
- 🏛 **Venue:** Salt Palace Convention Center
- 🔗 **Official website:** https://events.linuxfoundation.org/kubecon-cloudnativecon-north-america-2026/

Percona is a **Silver sponsor** at **KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America** (November 9-12) in Salt Lake City. We'll be in the **Solutions Showcase** with the cloud native community — alongside the projects and organizations building **Kubernetes**, cloud infrastructure, and distributed systems.

Our team is at the **Percona booth** and around the conference for practical conversations about running databases in cloud native environments: performance tuning, high availability, replication under load, backups, and architecture choices that hold up when you scale. Stop by if you have a question, or reach out ahead of time if you want to schedule time with us during the event.
