Percona is proud to sponsor PGConf.dev, taking place May 19–22 in Vancouver, reinforcing our strong commitment to the open source ecosystem. As a company deeply rooted in the PostgreSQL community, Percona is excited to support and engage with developers and contributors who are shaping the future of PostgreSQL.

- 📅 **Date/Time:** May 19, 2026 -> May 22, 2026
- 👤 **Conference URL:** https://2026.pgconf.dev
- 👤 **Location:**  Vancouver, Canada 

**PGConf.dev** in **Vancouver** (May 19–22) is one of the most important gatherings for PostgreSQL contributors, developers, and community leaders, and **Percona** is proud to be a sponsor of this highly technical and collaborative event. Percona deeply values its long-standing relationship with the **PostgreSQL** community and remains an active participant in helping drive innovation, stability, and performance across the ecosystem. Events like PGConf.dev provide a unique opportunity to connect directly with the people building PostgreSQL and to contribute meaningfully to its future.

Throughout the event, Percona experts will be on hand and eager to connect with attendees to discuss all things PostgreSQL—from performance tuning and scalability to best practices and real-world deployments. Be sure to catch Perconian **Andreas Karlsson** as he shares insights on the state of OAuth in PostgreSQL ([Are we OAuth yet?](/talks/2026/2026-05-19-are-we-oauth-yet/)), offering valuable perspective on a rapidly evolving area of the database landscape.
