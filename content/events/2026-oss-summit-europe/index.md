Percona is a **Silver sponsor** at **Open Source Summit Europe**, October 7-9 in **Prague**. We'll be in the exhibit hall with the open source community — talking databases, sharing what we know from production work, and catching up with contributors and operators face to face.

- 📅 **October 7-9, 2026**
- 📍 **Prague, Czechia**
- 🔗 **Official website:** https://events.linuxfoundation.org/open-source-summit-europe/

**Open Source Summit Europe** in **Prague** (October 7-9) brings together maintainers, contributors, and organizations working on open source across the stack. Percona is a **Silver sponsor** and a long-standing part of that community. We're there to collaborate with partners, share knowledge, and talk through the technologies that run modern applications.

Find us in the **exhibit hall**. Our team can go deep on **MySQL**, **PostgreSQL**, **MongoDB**, and **Valkey** — migrations, performance, replication, backups, and the operational problems you are dealing with now. Stop by the **Percona booth** if you have a question, want to compare notes on your database setup, or just want to say hello.
