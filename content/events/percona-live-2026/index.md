[Percona Live 2026](https://perconalive.com/) is back! Join the **open source database community** for three days of deep technical talks, hands-on workshops, and real conversations with people building and running MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MariaDB, Valkey, and more in production.

## When & where

- **May 27-29, 2026**
- **Computer History Museum**, Mountain View, California, 1401 N. Shoreline Blvd. Mountain View, California
- Venue Website: https://computerhistory.org/about/

![Percona Live Venue](/events/percona-live-2026/venue.jpg)

CFP (Call for Papers / Speakers)

- Session Formats:
    - 30-minute talks (+ Q&A time)
    - 3-hour hands-on workshops
