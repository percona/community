Discover how Percona’s latest **PostgreSQL Operator** feature transforms database backups with **Kubernetes VolumeSnapshots**. Learn the setup, benefits such as near-instant snapshots and improved disaster recovery, key limitations, and practical deployment steps for production workloads.


- 📅 **Date/Time:** 10 March, 13:00 PM GTM
- 👤 **Speaker:** Mayank Shah - Percona Senior Software Engineer
- 📍 Online
- 🔗 [Register here](https://event.on24.com/wcc/r/5268155/E0E4F92A432EE4C61301C598DE6F253F)

**Mayank** will share how Percona’s PostgreSQL Operator uses Kubernetes VolumeSnapshots to speed up backups, enable near-instant snapshots, and support disaster recovery. He’ll walk through the setup, key benefits, important limitations, and practical steps for deploying this in production.
