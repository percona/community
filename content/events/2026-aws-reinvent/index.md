Percona is a **Bronze sponsor** at **AWS re:Invent**, November 30-December 4 in **Las Vegas**. We'll be on the Expo floor with the cloud community — talking open source databases, performance tuning, and data infrastructure that holds up in AWS production environments.

- 📅 **November 30-December 4, 2026**
- 📍 **Las Vegas, Nevada**
- 🏛 **Venue:** Venetian Convention and Expo Center
- 🔗 **Official website:** https://aws.amazon.com/events/reinvent/

**AWS re:Invent** (November 30-December 4) in Las Vegas is one of the largest cloud conferences of the year — keynotes, deep technical sessions, and a full week of builders, engineers, and operators working across the AWS ecosystem. Percona is a **Bronze sponsor** again this year, on the **Expo floor** at the Venetian Convention and Expo Center alongside the teams shipping cloud infrastructure and open source data platforms.

Stop by the **Percona booth** to talk **MySQL**, **PostgreSQL**, **MongoDB**, and **Valkey** on AWS — performance, reliability, replication, backups, and cost tradeoffs you are actually weighing in production. Reach out ahead of time if you want to schedule a longer conversation with our team during the week.
