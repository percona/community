## Abstract

During this presentation, I will show how to implement several stuff that will improve your day-to-day workload.
The points will cover topics like Point in Time Recovery, WAN replication with group segments, Avoiding SST when adding new Percona XtraDB Cluster node, ... the tips will be briefly explained to the audience and then put in practice.
