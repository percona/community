## Abstract

Database complexity keeps escalating, but did you know there’s a way to simplify management and maximize performance?

Percona Monitoring and Management (PMM) is an open source database observability, monitoring, and management tool for MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB. PMM helps you simplify operations, optimize performance, and improve the security of your databases.

In this session, you will learn how to:
* install PMM
* register databases with it
* simplify operations and optimize performance
* improve the security of your database services
