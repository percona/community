When Redis changed its license, practitioners built [**Valkey**](https://valkey.io), a BSD-licensed fork with open governance under the Linux Foundation. Percona supports that path. We help teams run Valkey and Redis in production, contribute to the ecosystem, and show up where the community shares what actually works.

**Explore Percona's Valkey & Redis offerings:**

- [Valkey & Redis at Percona](https://www.percona.com/valkey-redis/) — how we support the ecosystem
- [Percona Valkey documentation](https://docs.percona.com/valkey/index.html) — guides and reference material

Questions about migration, operations, or where to start? Reach out to the team below. We'd love to hear what you're building.
